FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a FlashPay account? ▼
Download the FlashPay app, tap Register, enter your full name, email, phone number and create a 6-digit PIN. Your account is ready instantly!
Is FlashPay safe to use? ▼
Yes! FlashPay uses bank-level security including JWT authentication, bcrypt PIN encryption, biometric verification, PIN lockout after 5 wrong attempts, and AI-powered fraud detection.
How do I receive money on FlashPay? ▼
Every FlashPay user gets a unique dedicated Nigerian bank account number powered by our licensed banking partner. Your account number is shown on your dashboard and profile when you register. You can receive money from any Nigerian bank directly into your FlashPay wallet using this account number.
How long do transfers take? ▼
FlashPay to FlashPay transfers are instant, typically under 3 seconds. Bank transfers usually take 1-5 minutes depending on the recipient bank.
What are the transfer fees? ▼
FlashPay to FlashPay transfers are completely FREE. Bank transfers may attract a small fee depending on the amount. All fees are shown clearly before you confirm any transaction.
What do I do if I forget my PIN? ▼
Tap Forgot PIN on the login screen. Enter your registered phone number, receive an OTP on your email, verify it and create a new PIN. The process takes less than 2 minutes.
How do I earn FlashPoints? ▼
You earn FlashPoints automatically on every transaction: transfers, airtime purchases, bill payments, savings deposits and more. Points are credited instantly after each transaction.
