FlashPay was born from a simple belief: every Nigerian deserves access to fast, affordable and trustworthy financial services.

Our Story

From a phone in Ibafo to Nigeria

FlashPay started with a bold idea. What if one person could build a world-class fintech app using just a phone? That is exactly what happened in Ibafo, Ogun State, Nigeria.

Our founder, driven by the vision of making financial services accessible to every Nigerian, from market women in Lagos to traders in Kano, built FlashPay from scratch using a single Android phone in Termux.

What started as a personal project quickly became a mission: to build the financial operating system for Nigeria and eventually all of West Africa.

Today, FlashPay is a complete fintech super app with 20+ features, bank-level security, and a growing team ready to change how Nigerians interact with money.

Our Mission

Why we exist

Three core pillars drive everything we build at FlashPay.

🌍

Financial Inclusion

Nigeria has over 70 million unbanked adults. FlashPay exists to bring every single one of them into the digital economy with simple, accessible financial tools.

⚡

Speed & Simplicity

Financial transactions should be instant and effortless. We obsess over speed, reliability and simplicity so users can focus on what matters most, their lives and businesses.

🔐

Trust & Security

We handle people's money. That responsibility is sacred. Every feature we build is designed with security-first principles to protect every naira our users trust us with.

Our Journey

The FlashPay timeline

1
2025: The Beginning
FlashPay is conceived
The idea for FlashPay is born in Ibafo, Ogun State. A solo founder begins building Nigeria's next great fintech app using just an Android phone and Termux.
2
Early 2026: Building
Complete app built from scratch
20+ screens built, payment integrations with VTPass and Flutterwave, bank-level security implemented, and a beautiful UI/UX designed to rival leading Nigerian fintech apps.
3
Mid 2026: Registration
CAC Registration & Going Live
ESSENCE BUSINESS PLATFORM LIMITED registered with CAC. FlashPay goes live on VTPass, Flutterwave and Monnify with real transactions.
4
Late 2026: Launch
Public Launch & Google Play Store
FlashPay launches publicly on Google Play Store. Beta users onboarded. Team begins growing to support the expanding product.
5
2027: Growth
Expanding across Nigeria & West Africa
FlashPay expands to cover more Nigerian states, launches business banking, API platform, and begins West Africa expansion starting with Ghana.
