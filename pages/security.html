Security Features

Multiple layers of protection

Every FlashPay account is protected by multiple independent security systems working together.

bcrypt PIN Encryption

Your PIN is never stored in plain text. We use bcrypt hashing with salt rounds to make your PIN completely unreadable, even to our own team.

JWT Authentication

Every session uses industry-standard JSON Web Tokens that expire automatically after 7 days. No session, no access. Simple and secure.

HIGH SECURITY

Biometric Verification

Login and confirm transactions using your fingerprint or Face ID. Your biometric data is stored only on your device and never sent to our servers.

PIN Lockout System

After 5 incorrect PIN attempts, your account is automatically locked for 30 minutes. This prevents brute force attacks on your account.

CRITICAL PROTECTION
⏰

Auto Logout

FlashPay automatically logs you out after 5 minutes of inactivity. If someone picks up your unlocked phone, they cannot access your account.

HIGH SECURITY

Rate Limiting

Our servers limit the number of requests per minute from any single source. This blocks automated attacks, bots and hackers from bombarding our systems.

HIGH SECURITY

Defense in Depth

Stay Safe

Security tips for FlashPay users

Your security is a shared responsibility. Here are important tips to keep your account safe.

🤫

Never share your PIN

FlashPay staff will never ask for your PIN, password or OTP. Never share these with anyone, including family members or people claiming to be from FlashPay support.

📱

Enable biometric login

Turn on fingerprint or Face ID login in your Security Settings. Biometric login is faster and more secure than PIN login in most situations.

🔔

Review login notifications

FlashPay sends a notification every time your account is accessed. Review these alerts regularly and report any login you do not recognize immediately.

📲

Keep your app updated

Always install FlashPay updates as soon as they are available. Updates often include important security patches and improvements to protect your account.

🔐

Use a strong PIN

Avoid obvious PINs like 123456, 000000 or your birth year. Use a unique PIN that you do not use for any other app or service.

⚠️

Beware of phishing

Only download FlashPay from the official Google Play Store or App Store. Be suspicious of any link claiming to be FlashPay sent via WhatsApp, SMS or email.
